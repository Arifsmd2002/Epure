Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free carbon-neutral shipping on all furniture orders within Scandinavia and major European cities. Delivery typically takes 4-10 business days.

How do I care for my oak furniture?

Keep your pieces clean with a soft, lint-free cloth. We recommend applying our natural furniture oil twice a year to maintain the wood's luster and protection.

Do you provide design consultations?

Yes, we offer complimentary virtual design consultations with our Nordic design experts. Reach out via the form to book your session.

How is the sustainability score calculated?

Our scores are independently audited based on raw material origin, carbon footprint during production, fair labor practices, and recyclability of the piece.
